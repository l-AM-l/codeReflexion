First thoughts

In this project we were told to create a site based on one of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), our team decided on doing the SDG number 4, being the Quality and Education theme. We thought on plenty if ideas of what we could do but all the ideas that came to mind ended up being very average and done a lot of time before. That's we came to the idea to just make an educational website that has free accesss to veryone and learn basics skills at the comodity of your home so that all kinds od people depensing of any kind of situation can have the ability and more importantly free right to an education.
